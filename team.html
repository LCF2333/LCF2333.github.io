How We Accomplish Our Goal

Our team sits, tries, and reviews all espresso machines, we don't sell a machine without it passing our testing period. Here is a list of what we look for:

  • Good brewing
  • Affordable pricing
  • The quality of the machine
  • Amount of features included

Our Background

We all love coffee here, and wanted to share only the best of the best. We pride ourselves in the products we sell.

Brewed Awakening: Founders Story

Founder Bob Jenkins, founded Brewed Awakening in 1974, aiming to supply the worlds best espresso machines. He has since stepped down and passed the reins.

Brewed Awakening: Meet Our Current CEO

CEO Christopher Jenkins inherited Brewed Awakening from his father, and is always aiming to keep quality as his mission and made him proud.
